ONLINE HOUSE RENTAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM



TENANT

ONE WHO IS IN SEARCH OF HOUSE COME UNDER THIS. TO ACCESS THE SYSTEM THE INDIVIDUAL MUST HAVE AN ACCOUNT IN WHICH HE/SHE CAN SEARCH THE HOUSE OF THEIR REQUIREMENTS IN DIFFERENT LOCATIONS.


PROVIDER

ONE WHO UPLOADS DETAILS OF THE HOUSE COME UNDER THIS. TO ACCESS THE SYSTEM THE INDIVIDUAL MUST HAVE AN ACCOUNT IN WHICH HE/SHE CAN UPLOAD THE DETAILS OF THE HOUSE.


ADMIN

THE MAIN OPERATOR OF THE SYSTEM IS ADMIN. HE SAFEGUARDS THE INFORMATION STORED IN THE DATABASE AND UPDATE AS PER REQUIREMENTS.
